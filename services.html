Our Services


At MESCO we offer a wide array of Oilfield Services throughout West Texas and New Mexico. We take pride in offering unparalleled services to the Oil & Gas and Hydraulic Fracturing Industries. Our quality equipment and experienced team can help your company to get the job done safely and on-time.

Winch Trucks, Heavy Haul and Equipment Transport Services

MESCO is a leading provider of truck and heavy hauling services in the Permian Basin. At MESCO we have all the Winch Truck Services you need for your oilfield, construction projects and heavy-duty jobs.

  • Winch Truck Services

    • Our fleet of winch trucks include highly trained and skilled technicians to ensure that your services are completed on-time.

  • Oilfield Heavy Haul and Equipment Transport Services

    • Our trucks can load and transport your oilfield drilling equipment and frac tanks.

Frac Tank and Mud Pit Rentals

MESCO can manage your fluid with our large-volume Frac Tanks and Mud Pits. Our rental units hold up to 500 bbls for all your completion and production needs and are accompanied by a trained technician. We can move and transport Frac Tank and Mud Pit units to and from the site and takes care of hauling and disposal upon request.

On-Site Clean Up and Removal Services

MESCO provides on-site clean up and removal services. Our equipment and experienced crews can provide the necessary tools to contain any type of spill and emergency. When an immediate response is required, MESCO crews can improve safety, productivity, uptime and profitability on a 24/7 basis.

  • Equipment Cleaning

    • Additionally, our trained technicians and equipment provide on-site cleaning services to your heavy-duty vehicles and other oilfield equipment.
